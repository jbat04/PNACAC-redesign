Pacific NorthWest Association for College Admission Counseling

The Pacific Northwest Association for College Admission Counseling (PNACAC) represents college admissions professionals in Alaska, Idaho, Montana, Oregon and Washington - a region known for its beauty, friendliness and collaborative spirit.

A regional affiliate of the National Association for College Admission Counseling (NACAC), PNACAC is an education association of secondary school counselors, college and university admission officers and counselors and related individuals who work with students as they make the transition from high school to postsecondary education.

The Benefits of Membership

Join or renew PNACAC membership today!

PNACAC provides resources for its members to guide and empower students as they prepare for college admission. Becoming a member and getting involved is a great way to share your ideas with professionals throughout the Pacific Northwest, and country. Members gain access to:

  • Professional Development, including Annual Conference, Summer College Tour
  • Seven Regional College Fairs
  • Updates on Good Practices & National/Regional Issues through Committees and PNACAC Newsletter
  • The PNACAC Website, Membership List and Listserv to connect with colleagues
